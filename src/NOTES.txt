Some Notes for running MinCraft

To clean everything in the build, and refresh:

For IDEA:
./gradlew cleanCache clean setupDecompWorkspace --refresh-dependencies idea

For Eclipe:
./gradlew cleanCache clean setupDecompWorkspace --refresh-dependencies idea





Playing Micraft:
================

Open Inventory:
ctrl + E

To place Crafting Table:
ctrl + mouse click

To enter Crafting mode:

ctrl + mouse click
